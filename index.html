about me

Hi! My name is Kathy. I was born and raised in Los Angeles, CA. In 2016, I got my bachelor’s degree in Psychology with a minor in Korean Language from UCLA and spent 3 years post-grad working as a legal assistant at a employment litigation law firm in Brentwood. I always had an interest and passion for web development, and worked many years on individual projects teaching myself the skills I needed to change career paths. I eventually decided to take a leap of faith an enroll into a coding bootcamp program as a part-time student. Working fulltime and going to school at night and on the weekends was tough, but I knew it would all work out in the end. I’m always looking to sharpen my skills and take on new projects.


On my free time, I like to watch movies and hang out friends and family. Currently also trying to get back into reading!
